About

Born and raised in Boston, I'm a software engineer with a passion for developing innovative web applications and custom websites. Whether it's a sleek and professional business website or a fully interactive web app, I take pride in delivering high-quality, user-focused designs that meet my clients' needs.

I possess a wide range of skills, including expertise in React, TypeScript, Node.js, Modern JavaScript, HTML, Rails, CSS (and other style libraries, frameworks and preprocessors). My work is driven by a commitment to clean design and a seamless user experience.
